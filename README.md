# Diwali Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project performs an exploratory data analysis (EDA) on Diwali sales data to identify key customer demographics and popular product categories. The goal is to understand sales trends and customer behavior to help businesses make data-driven decisions.

## Data Source
The data used in this analysis is from the `Diwali Sales Data.csv` file, containing information about customer purchases during the Diwali festival.

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Insights

### Gender
*   Most buyers are females, and their purchasing power is greater than men.

### Age Group
*   The majority of buyers are females in the 26-35 age group.

### State
*   The highest number of orders and total sales/amount originate from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka.

### Marital Status
*   Married women show the highest purchasing power and are the most frequent buyers.

### Occupation
*   Buyers working in the IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors are the most active.

### Product Category
*   The most sold products fall under the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.

### Conclusion
Married women aged 26-35 years, primarily from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, and working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors, are most likely to purchase products from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories during Diwali sales.
